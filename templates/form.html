Assassinability




Instructions:

1. Add players. Make sure their phone# is preceded by a '1'.

2. Hit submit to start game.

3. Every player will get a target and a secret word.

4. Go assasin your target. Be careful not to get killed or share your secret word.

5. If you manage to kill your target, reply to the text you got by sending your target's secret word.

6. You will get a new target.

7. Keep on killing.

8. You will be notified when the game is over.
